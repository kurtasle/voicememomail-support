**Common Questions**

**How do I change my email address?**
Open the app and tap the Change Email icon to update your email.

**Why can't I send my recording?**
Make sure you have a mail account set up in your iPhone's Settings → Mail.

**Why is the app asking for microphone access?**
VoiceMemoMail needs your microphone to record voice memos. 
You can enable it in Settings → Privacy → Microphone.
