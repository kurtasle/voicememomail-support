Cup Curfew — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Cup Curfew is a caffeine tracking app that helps you know when to stop drinking coffee for better sleep. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed the app to collect as little data as possible.

Data Stored on Your Device

All of your caffeine tracking data — including drink logs, profile settings, bedtime preferences, and custom drinks — is stored locally on your device only using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any server.

Advertising

Cup Curfew displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data for ad personalization, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • General location (IP-based, not precise)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking). For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your caffeine tracking data
  • Your health information
  • Precise location data
  • Browsing history

No Servers

We do not operate any servers or cloud infrastructure connected to this app. All app functionality works entirely on your device.

Children's Privacy

Cup Curfew is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
